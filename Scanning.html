Scanning Techniques

Click here to return to the home page

  • Scan zone from bottom (deepest) to top (surface).
  • Make sure all parts of your designated zone are within view. Move positions if your view is obstructed by things like glare, objects (people/floats), waves, etc.
  • One full scan should not take more than 10 seconds. Make sure to monitor all areas of the zone, do not solely focus on areas with high activity.
  • Scan by moving your head, not just your eyes.
  • Pick a scanning pattern: right to left, left to right, diagonal, and change patterns frequently to stay alert.
  • Change positions every five minutes, either by standing up or walking around. Avoid sitting in one place for too long. When changing positions ensure your scanning does not falter.
  • Avoid distractions such as: talking to friends, looking at your phone, moving your mind off of the task at hand. No technology should be on the stand with you.
